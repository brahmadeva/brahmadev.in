significance: >-
  <p>This Shri Brahma Stotram is a devotional hymn of salutation to Brahma, the eternal, all-pervading and self-luminous Supreme, praised here as the sole cause, refuge and sustainer of the worlds.</p>
  <p>Its verses honour Brahma as the non-dual Truth beyond change and imagination, the greatest of lords and the God of gods, who is at once mother, father, kinsman, friend, knowledge and wealth to the devotee.</p>
  <p>Compact yet profound, it distils the essence of praise offered to the Creator as the innermost reality of all.</p>
benefits: >-
  <ul><li>Fosters an intimate sense of Brahma as mother, father, friend and refuge.</li><li>Bestows mental peace and steadiness through remembrance of the changeless Truth.</li><li>Deepens single-minded devotion to the one, formless Supreme.</li><li>Dispels fear, the hymn saluting the One who is the terror of terrors and the goal of all beings.</li><li>Nurtures the aspiration for liberation through surrender to the Creator.</li></ul>
recitationMethod: >-
  <p>Recite this stotram after bathing in the morning or at the evening twilight, seated in a clean place facing east or north with a calm and reverent mind.</p>
  <p>Chant it slowly and with feeling, reflecting on each salutation, and repeat it daily as a simple and heartfelt offering to Brahma.</p>
viniyog: >-
  <p>This is a general hymn of praise to Brahma as the Supreme Self; in the absence of a stated Rishi and Chhanda, its application is the daily devotional worship of the Creator for peace and liberation.</p>

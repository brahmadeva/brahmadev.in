significance: >-
  <p>This text, drawn from the Bramharchana Paddhati, sets out the traditional method (vidhi) for the japa of Lord Brahma's mantras. It preserves the consecration of the rosary, the revitalization (mantroddhara) of the mantra, the nyasa, and the principal Brahma mantra together with the Brahma Gayatri.</p>

# recitationMethod: >-
#   <p>First prepare a rosary (mala) of Rudraksha or red sandalwood beads. Before beginning, worship the mala with the prescribed verses so that it becomes a fit instrument for japa.</p>
#   <p>Next perform the mantroddhara and then the nyasa rites, placing the six mantra-syllables upon the fingers and hands (angadinyasa) and upon the heart, head, crest, and other points (hridayadinyasa) with the mantras given here. Only after this preparation should the mantra is to be recited.</p>
#   <p>Chant seated in a pure place after bath, at a fixed daily hour, keeping a steady count on the rosary and reciting according to one's chosen resolve. The traditional texts note that full accomplishment is described as arising through very prolonged and sustained repetition, so the practice is meant to be maintained regularly over a long period.</p>
# viniyog: >-
#   <p>The nyasa and mala worship given here form the practical viniyog of this japa, dedicating the rosary, the mantra, and the practitioner's own limbs to the deity so that the repetition is carried out with due sanctity before the desired aim is pursued.</p>

जपहेतु माला— <br/>
एतदनन्तर, आराधक को भगवान् देवाधिदेव ब्रह्माजी के अभीष्ट मन्त्र का न्यासादिपूर्वक रुद्राक्ष या लालचन्दन की माला पर स्वकार्यसिद्ध्यर्थ इच्छानुसार जप करना चाहिये । <br/>
<br/>
माला का पूजन — <br/>
सर्वप्रथम, माला का अधोलिखित मन्त्रों से पूजन कर जप प्रारम्भ करना चाहिये-

<br/>
Rosary worship –<br/>
First of all, one should start chanting the rosary by worshiping it with the following mantras-

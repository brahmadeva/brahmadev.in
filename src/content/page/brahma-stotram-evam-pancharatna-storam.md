titleDevanagri: श्री ब्रह्मस्तोत्रम् एवं पञ्चरत्नस्तोत्रं (महानिर्वाण तंत्रार्गत)
titleEnglish: Shri Brahma Stotram evam Pancharatna Storam (From Mahanirvan Tantra)

description: Shri Brahma Stotram from the Mahanirvan Tantra, is hymn spoken by Lord Shiva to the Goddess Parvati in praise of Brahman. The verses salute Brahma as the unequalled, formless and all-pervading Truth (Brahman) who creates, sustains and withdraws the worlds, and who bestows liberation upon the devotee. Its closing benediction declares that one who recites it with a pure heart attains Brahma-sayujya liberation (mukti), absorption into the Supreme.
